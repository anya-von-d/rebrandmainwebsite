Everything you need to know about using Vony for peer-to-peer lending.

Getting Started

Vony is a peer-to-peer lending platform designed for friends, family, and communities. It helps you create clear loan agreements, track repayments, and manage lending relationships without the awkwardness.

Vony offers a free tier that covers basic loan creation and tracking. Premium features like advanced analytics and priority support are available through our paid plans.

After signing up, tap "Create a Loan" from your dashboard. Enter the loan amount, set the repayment schedule, and optionally add interest. Then invite your borrower to review and sign the agreement.

Loans & Agreements

Vony agreements are designed to document the terms both parties agreed upon. While they serve as strong evidence of the arrangement, enforceability may vary by jurisdiction. We recommend consulting a legal professional for large amounts.
