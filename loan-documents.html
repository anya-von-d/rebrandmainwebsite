DOCUMENT 01

The Promissory Note

A legally recognized written promise from one person to pay another. Vony auto-generates it the moment both parties agree, including the principal, interest rate, repayment schedule, and digital signatures. No legal jargon, just a plain-language document that protects both sides.

Legally recognized in all 50 states
