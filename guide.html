Guides

Step-by-step tutorials to help you get the most out of Vony.

01

Getting Started with Vony

Create your account, set up your profile, and make your first loan in under 5 minutes.

1

Download Vony and create your account using email, Apple, or Google sign-in.

2

Complete your profile with a name and photo so friends can find you.

3

Connect a payment method under Settings > Payment Methods.

4

Tap "Create a Loan" on your dashboard to lend to or borrow from a friend.

02

Creating Your First Loan

A walkthrough of the loan creation process, from setting the amount to getting both parties to sign.

1

Choose whether you're lending or borrowing, then enter the amount.

2

Select or invite the other party by searching your contacts.

3

Set a repayment schedule: one-time, weekly, fortnightly, or monthly.

4

Optionally add interest, review the agreement summary, and send for approval.

03

Tracking Payments & Progress

Learn how to monitor repayments, view history, and stay on top of upcoming due dates.

1

Open any loan from your dashboard to see the progress bar and remaining balance.

2

Check the "History" tab for a detailed log of every payment made.

3

Use the Activity feed to see all transactions across your loans in one place.

4

Set custom reminders or use the default 3-day and 1-day due date alerts.

04

Managing Loan Agreements

How to view, modify, and share your loan contracts and digital agreements.

1

Tap any active loan and select "View Agreement" to see the full contract.

2

To make changes, tap "Request Change." The other party must approve.

3

Export the agreement as a PDF from the share menu for your records.

4

All changes are version-tracked, so you can view the full modification history.
