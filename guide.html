Guides

Step-by-step tutorials to help you get the most out of Vony.

Getting Started with Vony

Create your account, set up your profile, and make your first loan in under 5 minutes.

1

Sign up with your email, Apple, or Google account.

2

Add your name and profile photo so friends can find you.

3

Add a friend to get ready to create your first loan.

4

Tap "Create a Loan" on your dashboard to get started.

Creating Your First Loan

A walkthrough of the loan creation process, from setting the amount to getting both parties to agree.

1

Choose whether you are lending or borrowing, then enter the loan amount and a short reason.

2

Search for and select the other person from your friends list.

3

Set a repayment schedule: one-time, weekly, fortnightly, or monthly.

4

Review the loan summary and send it. The other party will need to accept before it becomes active.

Tracking Payments & Progress

Learn how to monitor repayments, view history, and stay on top of upcoming due dates.

1

Open any loan from your dashboard to see the remaining balance and repayment progress.

2

Tap into a loan and go to the History tab to see every payment made.

3

Visit Recent Activity to see all transactions across all your loans in one place.

4

Check Upcoming to see what payments are due and when.

Managing Loan Agreements

How to view and manage your loan contracts and documents.

1

Open any active loan and tap Documents to view the promissory note, amortisation schedule, or loan summary.

2

Download or share any document directly from the Documents page.

3

To request a change to a loan, both parties must agree: the other person will receive a notification to approve or decline.

4

All loan details are recorded at the time of creation so there is always a clear record for both parties.
