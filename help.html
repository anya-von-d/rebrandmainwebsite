Account & Setup

Download Vony from the App Store or Google Play, then tap "Sign Up." You can register with your email address or sign up using Apple, Google, or Facebook. Once verified, you'll be taken to your dashboard where you can start creating loans right away.

Go to Settings > Profile to add your name, photo, and a short bio. A complete profile helps friends recognise you when you send or receive loan requests. You can also set your preferred currency and time zone here.

Navigate to Settings > Payment Methods. Vony supports bank transfers, debit cards, and popular services like Apple Pay and Google Pay. All payment credentials are encrypted and never stored on our servers. You can add multiple methods and set a default.

Under Settings > Notifications, you can control which alerts you receive and how. Choose between push notifications, email, or SMS for payment reminders, loan updates, and friend requests. You can also enable quiet hours to pause non-urgent alerts.

Loans & Agreements

From your dashboard, tap "Create a Loan." Enter the loan amount, choose your borrower from your friends list (or invite them by email/phone), set a repayment schedule, and optionally add interest. Review the summary, then send it to the borrower for approval. Both parties must agree before the loan becomes active.

Every loan on Vony generates a digital agreement that outlines the amount, repayment schedule, interest (if any), and terms agreed upon by both parties. This agreement is timestamped and stored securely. While it serves as strong documentation, enforceability may vary by jurisdiction for large sums.

When creating a loan, choose between one-time, weekly, fortnightly, or monthly repayments. Set the start date and Vony will calculate the per-payment amount automatically. Both parties receive reminders before each due date. You can modify the schedule later if both parties agree.

Interest is entirely optional. If you choose to add it, Vony supports fixed-rate and simple interest. Enter the annual percentage rate and Vony calculates the total repayment amount. Both the lender and borrower see a transparent breakdown before signing the agreement.

Either party can propose a modification by tapping the loan and selecting "Request Change." You can adjust the amount, schedule, or interest rate. The other party must approve the change before it takes effect. All modifications are logged in the loan history for transparency.

Payments & Tracking

Open the loan from your dashboard and tap "Make Payment." Choose your payment method, confirm the amount, and submit. Payments are processed instantly and both parties receive a confirmation notification. You can also make early or partial payments at any time.

Each loan has a detailed "History" tab showing every payment made, when it was made, and the remaining balance. You can also view your overall payment history across all loans from the Activity tab on your dashboard, with filters for date range and status.

Vony sends automatic reminders 3 days and 1 day before a payment is due. You can customise the timing in Settings > Reminders. Lenders can also send a gentle nudge at any time by tapping "Send Reminder" on the loan page, which sends a friendly notification to the borrower.

If a payment is missed, Vony marks it as overdue and notifies both parties. There are no penalties by default, but the lender can set a grace period in the loan terms. After the grace period, the loan status changes to "Late" on both dashboards. Vony never reports to credit agencies.

Security & Privacy

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Payment credentials are tokenised through our payment partners and never stored on Vony's servers. We run regular security audits and penetration testing to ensure your information stays safe.

Enable 2FA in Settings > Security. Vony supports authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS codes. Once enabled, you'll need a verification code each time you log in or make a payment. We strongly recommend enabling 2FA to keep your account secure.

Under Settings > Privacy, you control who can find you by email or phone number, whether your profile is visible to non-friends, and how your data is used. You can download a copy of all your data or request account deletion at any time.

If you notice anything unusual on your account, tap the shield icon on any screen to report it immediately. You can also email security@vony.com. Our team investigates all reports within 24 hours and will temporarily lock your account if needed to protect your funds.
