When Traditional Loans Make Sense

Sometimes peer lending isn't the right fit. Here's when to consider other options.

Student Loans

Education costs beyond what friends can cover. Federal loans offer income-driven repayment and forgiveness programs.

Mortgage / Home Purchase

Home buying amounts are too large for peer lending. Mortgages offer 15-30 year terms at competitive rates.

Debt Consolidation

Combine multiple high-interest debts into one lower-rate payment to simplify your finances.

Emergency Expenses

Urgent medical bills or repairs when no one in your circle can help. Some lenders offer same-day funding.

Business Start-up

SBA and small business loans are built for entrepreneurs, with terms designed for business cash flow.

Personalised Recommendations, Free of Charge

Vony will assess your needs and give you tailored suggestions — whether that's lending through a friend on our platform or pointing you toward the right traditional option. No fees, no affiliate links, no hidden agenda. Just honest guidance.
